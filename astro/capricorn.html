Overview

Capricorn is the tenth sign of the zodiac, associated with ambition, responsibility, and long-term success. Ruled by Saturn, this sign represents endurance through time.

Personality

Capricorns are practical and self-controlled, often misunderstood as distant. Beneath the surface is loyalty, humor, and emotional depth earned through trust.
