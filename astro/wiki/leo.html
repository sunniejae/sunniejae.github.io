Sign Overview

Leo radiates warmth, confidence, and creative vitality. Born performers and natural leaders, Leos possess magnetic charisma that draws others into their orbit. Generous and big-hearted, they love to inspire and entertain, often taking center stage with their dramatic flair and sunny disposition. Their pride and dignity are essential to their identity, and they approach life with regal confidence and unwavering loyalty to those they love.

Strengths

  • Confident and charismatic
  • Generous and warm-hearted
  • Creative and passionate
  • Natural leadership qualities
  • Loyal and protective
  • Optimistic outlook
  • Inspiring and motivational

Weaknesses

  • Egotistical and attention-seeking
  • Stubborn and inflexible
  • Domineering tendencies
  • Overly dramatic
  • Sensitive to criticism
  • Extravagant spending
  • Needs constant validation
