How to Order

Exclusive to Order Items

  1. Browse the "Exclusive to Order" section
  2. Click "Add to Wishlist" on items you're interested in
  3. Click the wishlist icon (♡) in the header to view your selections
  4. Fill in your name and email
  5. Click "Request Order" to send your wishlist via email
  6. We'll respond with availability and payment details

Direct Order Items

For items in the "Direct Order" section, simply click the "Order Now" button to be taken directly to the retailer's website where you can complete your purchase immediately.
